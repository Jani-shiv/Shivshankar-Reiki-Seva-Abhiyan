Frequently Asked Questions

What is Reiki exactly?

Reiki is a Japanese energy healing technique. The practitioner transfers universal energy through their palms to the patient in order to encourage emotional or physical healing.

Is Reiki safe?

Yes, Reiki is completely safe, non-invasive, and gentle. It works in harmony with other medical or therapeutic techniques to relieve side effects and promote recovery.

Do I need to believe in it for it to work?

No. Reiki works whether you believe in it or not, similar to how meditation or yoga has physiological effects regardless of belief. However, being open to the experience helps.
